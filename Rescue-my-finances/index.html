Rescue my finances!

A quick and easy solution to manage your money.

Role: UX/UI Designer

Overview:

"Rescue My Finances!" Is a solution to one of the biggest problems in people's finances, financial management. Managing people's finances quickly, efficiently and inexpensively.

Skills and technologies:

Figma, HTML, CSS, UI Design, UX Writing & Research.

Work performed:

UX Research, interface design for mobile applications and responsive web pages, testing, prototyping.

May 2024 - In progress

The process:

We apply the "Design thinking" method, which consists of 5 stages:

  • Research.
  • Definition.
  • Ideation.
  • Prototyping.
  • Evaluation.

Why don't we start designing directly? Because we always have to focus our designs on people, we don't work to make the most beautiful page or application... We work to provide the best possible experience to users and for that we must know them.

Research or empathize:

In this stage we define the main idea, competence, context, create hypotheses, explore complementary ideas, conduct surveys and interviews.

All this to know our target audience and ensure that our idea can help them.

Many people make the mistake of spending weeks, months or even years developing an idea that no one really needs... We must be aware of the needs and desires of the people, as well as the competition, the context and the possible improvements of our project. Why? Simple, users are our main source of income, if we offer them a product they don't want they will ignore us.

Research can be tedious and unattractive from many points of view but I assure you that nothing is worth more than a product made especially for people.

By the way I will show you some images that represent a small part of each stage, if you want to see the complete process ask me for the file.

Definition:

We establish our target audience, their needs, problems and desires... In addition to briefly and effectively raise the main problem.

This last action will help us to create smarter ideas to achieve our goal... As Albert Einstein said: "If I had one hour to solve a problem, and my life depended on the solution, I would spend the first 55 minutes in determining the appropriate question, because once I knew the right question, I could solve the problem in less than five minutes".

Ideation:

Based on the previous stages we created and evaluated multiple ideas to achieve our objective, identifying their strengths, weaknesses, the value they bring to people, their profitability and the ease of implementation of each proposal.

In turn we write the "attractive text" that attracts users to our idea, in addition to creating the scripts, flowcharts and define in which platform the proposal will be implemented (mobile, website, etc).

Prototyping:

Start the design! As we move forward, the UI kit is created and first of all... I always seek to apply Dieter Rams' principles and deliver a product that is really useful for people.

Test:

As its name suggests, this phase is based on testing our idea and, depending on the results obtained, applying corrections or improvements.

It should be noted that the tests should be performed by people who have no experience with our idea, i.e. anyone who is not linked to the project... Why? Because we created that proposal and that blinds us from many mistakes.

For example, imagine that you drew a dog and from your perspective you know perfectly well that it is a dog but the others can not appreciate it clearly... This error is very common and is catastrophic for the usability of the user.

For this reason you should always look for people outside the project to test your idea, either friends, family or you can also hire people for it (if you ask yourself the question, it is not expensive)... At the same time, I always carry with me an "anti-suit" contract, to protect the integrity of the person and the idea.
